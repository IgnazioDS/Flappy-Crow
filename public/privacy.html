Flappy Crow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-24

Overview

Flappy Crow does not require an account and does not collect personal information. Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device.

Data Stored on Device

  • Settings (audio, accessibility, theme, and control preferences)
  • Scores, run history, and replay data
  • Coins, inventory, and daily reward streak state
  • Purchase entitlements (e.g., Remove Ads, Supporter Pack)

Optional Analytics

Analytics are opt-in. If you enable telemetry, the game may send anonymized gameplay events (such as score milestones or feature usage) to the configured analytics provider. You can disable analytics at any time in Settings.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by the App Store. We do not receive or store payment details. Purchase status is stored locally so the game can unlock cosmetic content and remove ads where applicable.

Contact

For questions or support, visit support.html or open an issue on GitHub.
